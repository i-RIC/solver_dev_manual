cg_iric_read_bc_functionalsize
================================

関数型の境界条件の変数のサイズを読み込む。

---------------
.. code-block:: fortran

   call cg_iric_read_bc_functionalsize(fid, type, num, name, size, ier)

形式 (C/C++)
---------------
.. code-block:: c

   ier = cg_iRIC_Read_BC_functionalSize(fid, type, num, name, &size);

形式 (Python)
